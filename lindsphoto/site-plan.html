FAQ

What are your prices?

- Portrait photography: $50 for an hour, includes 10 edited images
- Family photos: $100 that includes 5 edited group photos, 3 portraits of each family member (for families with 6 members or less)
- Engagements: $80 for one hour with 15 edited photos
- Pet and Newborn Photos: $30 for one hour, includes 7 edited photos
- Contact me for questions about any other needed photos!

Where are you willing to go to take photos?

I am willing to travel anywhere within reason! Locations that require extensive travel may require reimbursement for fuel.

What does your availability usually look like?

It really depends. As with most questions, you can contact me for more specific dates but I am generally booked three times a week.

What is your favorite thing to photograph?

I love doing any kind of portrait photography!

About Me

Welcome to my site! I'm so excited to meet you all. My name is Lindsay Green and I am 22 years old. I was born and raised in Orem, Utah and lived there all of my life until I moved away to go to college at Brigham Young University-Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho the September after graduating high school in 2019. I began my photography journey around the age of 15 when I was in junior high because my older brother had taken an interest in it and I wanted to be just like him. After that point, I took a few photography classes and developed my own photography style and began taking photographs for family and friends. People seemed to like my work, so I decided to make something that had been my side hobby into something I could pursue as a career and I think it has worked out pretty well so far! Other than photography, I enjoy drawing, painting, knitting and crocheting, reading, and going on hikes. If there's anything else you would like to know about me, feel free to contact me! :)
