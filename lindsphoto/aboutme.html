About Me

Welcome to my site! I'm so excited to meet you all. My name is Lindsay Green and I am 22 years old. I was born and raised in Orem, Utah and lived there all of my life until I moved away to go to college at Brigham Young University-Idaho in Rexburg, Idaho the September after graduating high school in 2019.

I began my photography journey around the age of 15 when I was in junior high because my older brother had taken an interest in it and I wanted to be just like him. After that point, I took a few photography classes and developed my own photography style and began taking photographs for family and friends.

People seemed to like my work, so I decided to make something that had been my side hobby into something I could pursue as a career and I think it has worked out pretty well so far! Other than photography, I enjoy drawing, painting, knitting and crocheting, reading, and going on hikes. If there's anything else you would like to know about me, feel free to contact me! :)
